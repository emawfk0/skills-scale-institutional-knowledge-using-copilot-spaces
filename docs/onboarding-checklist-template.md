# OctoAcme New Team Member Onboarding Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist ensures consistent, thorough onboarding for all new team members joining OctoAcme projects. The Onboarding Coordinator uses this template to track progress and ensure nothing is missed.

---

## Pre-Arrival (Before Day 1)
**Owner: Onboarding Coordinator**

- [ ] Welcome email sent with start date, time, and logistics
- [ ] Equipment and accounts provisioned (laptop, email, GitHub, tools)
- [ ] Onboarding schedule shared (meetings, introductions, training)
- [ ] Buddy/mentor assigned
- [ ] Access to documentation repository and Copilot Spaces

---

## Week 1: Orientation & Setup

### Day 1
**Owner: Onboarding Coordinator + Buddy**

- [ ] Welcome meeting with team lead and onboarding coordinator
- [ ] Office/workspace tour (physical or virtual)
- [ ] Review of company values, mission, and culture
- [ ] IT setup and tool configuration assistance
- [ ] Introduction to immediate team members
- [ ] Review onboarding schedule and expectations

### Days 2-5
**Owner: Onboarding Coordinator**

- [ ] Complete HR paperwork and compliance training
- [ ] Review OctoAcme project management overview ([docs/README.md](README.md))
- [ ] Study relevant role persona ([octoacme-roles-and-personas.md](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md))
- [ ] Explore project lifecycle documentation
- [ ] Shadow a daily standup or team meeting
- [ ] Introduction to key stakeholders and cross-functional partners
- [ ] Set up development environment (if applicable)
- [ ] Review team communication channels and etiquette

---

## Week 2: Learning & Context

**Owner: Buddy/Mentor with Onboarding Coordinator support**

- [ ] Deep dive into current projects and roadmap with Product Manager
- [ ] Review recent retrospectives and learnings
- [ ] Walkthrough of key systems, tools, and workflows
- [ ] Attend project planning or sprint planning session
- [ ] Review risk register and communication strategies ([octoacme-risks-and-communication.md](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md))
- [ ] First small task or bug fix assigned (if applicable)
- [ ] Mid-onboarding check-in with Onboarding Coordinator

---

## Week 3-4: Hands-On Contribution

**Owner: Project Manager + Buddy/Mentor**

- [ ] Participate in code/design reviews (observe and contribute)
- [ ] Lead a small, well-defined task or feature
- [ ] Attend release or deployment (observe process)
- [ ] Join retrospective and contribute feedback
- [ ] Begin contributing to team rituals (standups, demos)
- [ ] Ask questions and document learnings

---

## Day 30: Onboarding Completion

**Owner: Onboarding Coordinator**

- [ ] 30-day check-in meeting with team lead and Onboarding Coordinator
- [ ] Onboarding feedback survey completed
- [ ] Review progress on initial goals and responsibilities
- [ ] Identify any remaining gaps or support needs
- [ ] Celebrate successful onboarding!

---

## Continuous Support (Beyond Day 30)

**Owner: Project Manager + Team**

- [ ] Regular 1:1s with manager established
- [ ] Ongoing learning and development plan discussed
- [ ] Integration into team planning and decision-making
- [ ] Feedback loop for ongoing improvements to onboarding process
